# GTIL League Rules & Structure

## League Structure

### Tiers & Splits

GTIL operates on a three-tier system with promotion and relegation:

| Tier | Position Range | Split Name | Drivers |
|------|---------------|------------|---------|
| Tier 1 | Positions 1–14 | Split 1 | Top 14 |
| Tier 2 | Positions 15–28 | Split 2 | Middle 14 |
| Tier 3 | Positions 29–41 | Split 3 | Bottom 13 |

### Promotion & Relegation

- **Bottom 3** of Tier 1 → Relegated to Tier 2
- **Top 3** of Tier 2 → Promoted to Tier 1
- **Bottom 3** of Tier 2 → Relegated to Tier 3
- **Top 3** of Tier 3 → Promoted to Tier 2

Moves occur at the end of each season (post-Grand Final).

### Multipliers

- **Sprint Race:** 1× (standard points)

## Race Formats

### Sprint Race
- Duration: ~20 minutes
- Format: Short race, standard points
- No tire wear/fuel strategy required

### Race 100
- Duration: ~50 minutes
- Format: 100% race distance
- Double points
- May require tire wear and fuel strategy

### Double Points Round
- Duration: ~25–35 minutes
- Format: Extended race
- 2× points multiplier

## Car Regulations

### Gr.3 (GT3)
- Weight: 1,200–1,400 kg
- Power: 500–600 HP
- Balance of Performance (BoP) active

### Gr.4 (GT4)
- Weight: 1,100–1,300 kg
- Power: 350–450 HP
- BoP active

### Gr.1 (Prototype)
- Hybrid and non-hybrid variants
- LMP1 / Hypercar class

### Special Car Restrictions

Some rounds have specific car restrictions (see schedule):

- **No BMW** (R3 - Dragon Trail Gardens)
- **No Citroen/Genesis** (R4 - Sardegna A)
- **German make only** (R7 - Nürburgring GP)
- **American make only** (R10 - Road Atlanta)
- **Japan make only** (R15 - Fuji Speedway)
- **MR drivetrain only** (R14, R17 - Mount Panorama, Yas Marina)

## Racing Etiquette & Penalties

### General Conduct

1. **Clean Racing** — No intentional contact or weaving
2. **Defensive Lines** — One move only, no blocking
3. **Corner Exit** — Leave space for overtaking car
4. **Blue Flags** — Yield to faster drivers when lapped

### Penalties

| Infraction | Time Penalty | Points Deduction |
|-------------|--------------|------------------|
| First-corner contact | +5s | Warning |
| Blocking / weaving | +10s | -5 pts |
| Dangerous re-entry | +15s | -10 pts |
| Revenge contact | +30s | -25 pts |
| Repeat offenses | Disqualification | -50 pts |

### Penalty Review

All incidents reviewed by stewards post-race. Appeals must be submitted within 24 hours of results posting.

## Race Weekend Procedure

### Pre-Race (Day of Event)
- **Room Opens:** 30 minutes before race time
- **Qualifying:** 10-minute session, grid set by fastest lap
- **Formation Lap:** 1 lap, no overtaking until start line

### During Race
- **Pit Stops:** Optional for Sprint/Race 100 (no limit)
- **Tires:** Compounds per track rules
- **Fuel:** Unlimited, must complete race distance

### Post-Race
- **Results:** Posted within 1 hour
- **Stewards Review:** 2–4 hours
- **Final Results:** Published after review

---

## Connection & DNF Policies

### Connection Issues

- **Pre-race disconnection:** Can rejoin if within 5 minutes of qualifying start
- **During race:** No rejoin — DNF awarded
- **Multiple DNFs:** May result in tier demotion at season end

### DNS (Did Not Start)

- Notify league admin at least 1 hour before race
- No penalty for up to 3 DNS per season
- 4+ DNS → Tier demotion consideration

---

## League Administration

### Responsibilities

- **Race Director:** Oversees race day operations, penalty reviews
- **Stewards:** Review incidents, apply penalties
- **Tier Managers:** Manage promotions/demotion, driver communication

### Communications

- **Primary:** Discord server (GTIL League)
- **Results:** Posted in #race-results channel
- **Discussions:** #general channel

---

## Code of Conduct

1. **Respect all drivers** — No harassment or toxic behavior
2. **Accept penalties gracefully** — Appeals process is fair and transparent
3. **Communicate delays** — Notify admin if unable to attend
4. **No external tools** — Use only in-game aids allowed by GT

### Violations

First offense: Warning
Second offense: Points deduction
Third offense: Suspension
Severe violations: Permanent ban
